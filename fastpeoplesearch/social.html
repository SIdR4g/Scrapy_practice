Social Mentions Search and Media Monitoring

\n
Real-time social media search allows you to quickly pull all recent mentions from the major Social Networks and Web into one dashboard to streamline your online presence tracking efforts and be a huge time saver.

Start Searching
Input desired keywords or hashtags into the search input and explore recently published posts, which contain their mentions.
Query syntax supports exact phrase search via putting double quotes around a set of words ( \xe2\x80\x9cexact phrase\xe2\x80\x9d ), minus keywords via attaching a minus sign immediately before a word ( -adult ) and OR operator (note that you have to type \xe2\x80\x98OR\xe2\x80\x99 in ALL CAPS).
Advanced search configuration is also available under the \xe2\x80\x9cSearch Settings\xe2\x80\x9d menu.

Email Alerts
Enable email alerts to keep track on what people are saying about your company, brand, product, or service.
No more worries that important customer feedback will be missing, we\xe2\x80\x99ve got you covered, just set up email alerts and be notified about new social mentions.

Premium Monitoring
Start collecting full history of your Brand mentions across the entire web and social media, your data is stored as long as your subscription is active.
Effectively navigate and edit found posts: sort by popularity to highlight the most important posts, filter search results by the predefined time periods, search within the mentions history, mark posts as favourite, delete irrelevant posts and change sentiment metric if needed.
Programmatic API access to all features of the website to fuel your internal tools and services.

Social Mentions Search and Media Monitoring

\r\n
Real-time social media search allows you to quickly pull all recent mentions from the major Social Networks and Web into one dashboard to streamline your online presence tracking efforts and be a huge time saver.

Start Searching
Input desired keywords or hashtags into the search input and explore recently published posts, which contain their mentions.
Query syntax supports exact phrase search via putting double quotes around a set of words ( \xe2\x80\x9cexact phrase\xe2\x80\x9d ), minus keywords via attaching a minus sign immediately before a word ( -adult ) and OR operator (note that you have to type \xe2\x80\x98OR\xe2\x80\x99 in ALL CAPS).
Advanced search configuration is also available under the \xe2\x80\x9cSearch Settings\xe2\x80\x9d menu.

Email Alerts
Enable email alerts to keep track on what people are saying about your company, brand, product, or service.
No more worries that important customer feedback will be missing, we\xe2\x80\x99ve got you covered, just set up email alerts and be notified about new social mentions.

Premium Monitoring
Start collecting full history of your Brand mentions across the entire web and social media, your data is stored as long as your subscription is active.
Effectively navigate and edit found posts: sort by popularity to highlight the most important posts, filter search results by the predefined time periods, search within the mentions history, mark posts as favourite, delete irrelevant posts and change sentiment metric if needed.
Programmatic API access to all features of the website to fuel your internal tools and services.
